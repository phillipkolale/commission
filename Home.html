THE PRESIDENT

His Excellency Dr. William Samoei Ruto, C.G.H., President of the Republic of Kenya and Commander-in-Chief of the Defence Forces.

His Excellency Dr William Samoei Ruto was sworn in on September 13, 2022, after winning the Presidential election. He was elected on August 9, 2022 on a United Democratic Party (UDA) ticket, a principal party, in the Kenya Kwanza Alliance.

THE HIGH COMMISSIONER H.E. AMB. (HON) MOHAMED M. SHIDIYE

Welcome to the official website of the High Commission of the Republic of Kenya to Botswana. We hope that you will find this website and linkages useful to get information and news on Kenya as well as Kenya/Botswana bilateral relations.

read more

The Mission was opened as a consulate in December 1997 following a request by the Kenyan Community in Botswana to former President Daniel Arap Moi during his state visit to Botswana and later upgraded to a fully-fledged High Commission in 2001.
Its mandate is to implement Kenya’s foreign policy and strengthen bilateral relations with Botswana in all of its aspects. The High Commission is also accredited to the Southern African Development Community (SADC) whose headquarters is in Gaborone. The current High Commissioner is Her Excellency Amb. Jean W. Kimani.
